A Look at ECMAScript's Iterator Helper Methods — The proposal is several years old, but now at stage 3 in TC39, Iterator Helpers are being implemented and ship with V8 12.2/Chrome 122. The helpers are functions like .map, .filter, .take and .forEach and are made available to any object with Iterator.prototype in its prototype chain.
